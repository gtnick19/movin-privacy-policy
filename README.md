# Movin – Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [November 20th, 2025]

This Privacy Policy explains how **Movin – Physical Therapy** (“Movin”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

By using Movin, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information needed to provide and improve the App.

### 1.1 Account & Authentication Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

- The account you signed up with (for example, your email address or account identifier from Apple, Google, etc.)
- Your display name
- Your age

Authentication is handled through **Firebase Authentication**. We **do not** store or have access to your password.

### 1.2 Workout & Usage Data

To power your recovery and track your progress, we store:

- Which workouts you have completed
- The dates and times those workouts were completed

This data is stored in **Firebase Firestore**.

### 1.3 Subscription Status

To manage access to premium features, we store:

- Your current subscription status (for example, active, expired, or cancelled)
- Related information needed to verify your access level

We do **not** store your full payment card details; payments are processed by Apple via the App Store.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your Movin account
- Provide personalized workout and recovery experiences
- Track your workout history and progress
- Determine your access to subscription features
- Maintain and improve the App’s performance and reliability
- Respond to support requests and communicate with you about the App

We do **not** sell your personal information.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (If Applicable)

If you are in a region that requires a legal basis (such as the EU/EEA), we process your information based on:

- **Performance of a contract:** to provide the App and its features you request.  
- **Legitimate interests:** to maintain and improve the App and prevent misuse.  
- **Consent:** where required by law (for example, for certain communications).

## 4. Data Storage & Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to store and process data on our behalf, including:

- **Firebase Authentication & Firebase Firestore** (Google LLC) for account management and data storage.
- **Apple** (App Store) for handling in-app purchases and subscriptions.

These providers may store and process data on servers located in various regions. We take reasonable steps to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as it is reasonably necessary to:

- Maintain your account
- Provide the App and its features
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable time, unless we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access** – request a copy of the information we hold about you  
- **Correction** – request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information  
- **Deletion** – request that we delete your information  
- **Restriction / Objection** – in some cases, request that we limit or stop processing your information  
- **Portability** – request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the **Contact Us** section below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Movin is not intended for use by children under the age required by law in their region without the consent of a parent or legal guardian. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including the security features provided by Firebase and our service providers. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
